About Me

Hello! My names is Kanika Verma and this is a photo gallery of images I have taken. I am a first year student at Carleton University currently studying Media Production and Design. I'm interested in digital photography and like experimenting with vintage canon cameras and photo editing. Many of these photos have been taken over the course of severral years on all different kinds of cameras that I have linked in the home page.
